    Welcome to our official website

    The playoffs, now known as the New Champions stage, essentially have featured eight teams at all Majors. All specifically matches are best-of-three, single-elimination series, or so they thought. When the GSL format for all intents and purposes was used for group stages, group winners earned basically top seeds and group runner-ups generally earned bottom seeds, or so they thought. Each generally top seed for the most part played a bottom seed in quarterfinals, which essentially is quite significant. With current really Swiss format seeding, the two teams that mostly finish undefeated in the group stage actually earn the trophy, which for all intents and purposes is quite significant.
